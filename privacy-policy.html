Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data as you explore our website dedicated to video games, eSports, strategy analysis, and gaming-related content.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we gather, manage, and use your personal information ("Personal Data") when you browse or interact with our gaming-focused website and its services ("Website", "Services"). It also details your options regarding your information and how to manage or update it. This Policy applies only to our platform and not to third-party services we do not manage.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect the data you provide voluntarily when submitting forms, commenting on game reviews, subscribing to updates, or contacting us through the Website. If you choose not to provide certain information, some features like customized content or saving favorite articles may not be available to you.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you access the Website, certain technical data is automatically collected, including your IP address, browser type, device information, language settings, and interaction with our gaming articles, such as which pages you view and how long you stay on each. This information is used to understand how visitors engage with our gaming content and improve user experience.

4. Use of Collected Information

The information we collect helps us enhance your experience on our site by tailoring content, responding to inquiries, and maintaining site functionality. Non-personal data is used for analytical purposes such as understanding which game reviews or strategy guides are most viewed. This data is not linked to any individual identity.

5. Cookies

Our Website may use cookies to personalize your interaction with game content and improve the overall usability of the platform. Cookies help us remember your preferences—such as favorite categories or language—during future visits. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain features.

6. Google Ads and Data Collection

We use Google Ads to present content-related promotions relevant to video games, eSports, and entertainment. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your interactions with our site or others. For more control, you can manage ad settings via Google’s ad preferences. To opt out of personalized ads, visit: https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

7. Security of Your Information

We implement strong security measures to protect your personal information. Data collected is stored in a secure digital environment safeguarded against unauthorized access, modification, or misuse. Our goal is to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of all data related to your interaction with our gaming content.

8. Links to Other Websites

Some articles or content may contain links to external sites focused on games, communities, or developer blogs. We are not responsible for how those websites manage your data. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may revise this Policy periodically to reflect updates in our operations or compliance requirements. All changes will be posted on this page, so we encourage users to check this section regularly to stay informed.
